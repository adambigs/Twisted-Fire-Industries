Meet the Team

Joe Yowler
Co-Founder

Joe is a second-generation fire fighter and has been in the fire service for 14 years. He is co-founder of Twisted Fire Industries & Tactics on Tap Metrolina. Joe is also a task force leader for Sons of the Flag (NC). Outside of the fire service, he enjoys spending time & traveling with his wife and two daughters (14 & 5). Joe has his hands in a little bit of everything. There is not a skill that he has not at least been exposed to, he is truly a jack of all trades. He is a great father, husband, and an even better friend. Joe is the kind of guy you know you can call at any time of the day and he will be there for you even if he’s giving you a ton of sarcasm before helping you out. Being able to bring all the different walks of life that make up the Twisted Fire Cadre says a lot about his ability to make people feel welcome and a part of something.

Heather Yowler
Fire Family Advocate

Heather is an advocate for fire families. She is speaking out to get families more involved with the fire service and their training. Heather has been a fire wife for 7 years and lived the “Fire Life” for 16. She’s the chaos coordinator of the fire training company, Twisted Fire Industries and Tactics on Tap Metrolina. In her spare time she enjoys gardening, wine with friends and traveling with her husband and their 2 daughters.

Our Vision

At Twisted Fire Industries, we will share a vision that provides an aggressive, tried and true education experience. We will accomplish this through innovation, progressiveness, preparation and dedication. We are professionals who encourage open and honest communication while taking pride in the heritage of the fire service.
